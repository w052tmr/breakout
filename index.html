breakout!

How to play

  • 1.) Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle horizontally and hit the ball.
  • 2.) Deflect the ball from your paddle at an angle that will hit and break as many bricks as possible. You get 1 point for each brick that you break.
  • 3.) Be careful though... If you miss the ball, your score will reset to 0, and the number of bricks will reset as well.
  • 4.) Good luck!
